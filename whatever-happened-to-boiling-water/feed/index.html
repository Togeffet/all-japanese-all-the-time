Fortunately, its not an either/or, and they compliment each other perfectly. Almost like…insulation and a pot of boiling water.

]]> By: kendo http://www.alljapaneseallthetime.com/blog/whatever-happened-to-boiling-water/#comment-59206 Mon, 01 Nov 2010 00:26:53 +0000 http://www.alljapaneseallthetime.com/blog/?p=3127#comment-59206 One element missing in your metaphor is the SRS. Keeping up with your daily SRS reps is like buying more insulation for the pan. Nope, it will NEVER make the water boil on its own. But it drastically decreases the amount of heat and the amount of time needed to bring the water to boil in an almost indirect way. Sure, for example, you could just do input, frequently looking up words you don’t know in the dictionary, and eventually, with enough input, close enough together, for a long enough period of time, the water will boil. But, by keeping those words you look up in the SRS, and keeping them in long term memory through your reviews, you only have to look that word up the first time. And you benefit from that single look-up more immediately, getting more out of your input right away. The SRS can’t ever replace good old fashioned books, audio and video, but it damn sure supplements the process nicely.
