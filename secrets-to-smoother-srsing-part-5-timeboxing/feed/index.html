]]> By: Squintox http://www.alljapaneseallthetime.com/blog/secrets-to-smoother-srsing-part-5-timeboxing/#comment-10820 Mon, 22 Sep 2008 12:21:03 +0000 http://www.alljapaneseallthetime.com/blog/?p=306#comment-10820 I recommend anyone using Anki to download the RLC Blitzer Plug-in. It’s a plug-in that can help you “timebox”.

Say, you open Anki, and have 300 reviews, initially you do 10 reps. And then the Blitzer asks if you want to keep going; “5 more cards”, “1 more card”, “Enough”.
