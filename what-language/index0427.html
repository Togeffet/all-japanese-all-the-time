What Language Are You Interested In?

While this site is primarily for and about learning Japanese, because learning principles transfer so readily (indeed, many of the techniques you’ll find on this site were originally drawn from or inspired by the AntiMoon method, which was developed by a coupla guys in Poland for the purpose of learning English), and because people who learn one new language tend to be greedy for more (“language lust”), there is some material that applies more generally to other languages as well as more specifically to languages other than Japanese.

So, that’s why a site called “All Japanese All The Time” can end up unironically asking you the question: “what language are you interested in?”. And you can answer that question by clicking on the appropriate image below…　↓↓↓↓↓↓↓↓↓↓
