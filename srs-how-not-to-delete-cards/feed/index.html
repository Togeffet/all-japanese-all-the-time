]]> By: ijp http://www.alljapaneseallthetime.com/blog/srs-how-not-to-delete-cards/#comment-1000053986 Fri, 12 Jul 2013 06:27:40 +0000 http://www.alljapaneseallthetime.com/blog/?p=25548#comment-1000053986 I went through RTK 3 times, and I started from scratch each time. My reasoning being: “It was so long ago, I’ll never remember anything, and oh look how many reps I have to do”. I expect this is one of the common reasons for “kill em all, god will know his own” deletion.

And the take home message of my last Japanese existential crisis was that it’s complete bull.

You don’t have to do all those reps at once, take a few weeks. By the end of that time, your SRS will be back to normal, and the cards you’ve forgotten will have been rescheduled appropriately.

As an aside, the Anki guy should sell “your SRS knows better than you” T-shirts, mine certainly knows better than me.
